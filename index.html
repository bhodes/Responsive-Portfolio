About Me

My name is Billy Hodes and I am 21 years old. I was born in Fairhope, Alabama and have lived in Kansas City, Missouri since I was 1. I graduated from Rockhurst High School in 2015 and went on to study civil engineering at the University of Missouri. After two years at Mizzou, I decided that civil engineering was not for me and returned home.

I am currently looking to become a web developer and am enrolled in a coding bootcamp at KU. My interests and hobbies include painting, sports, traveling, and reading.

I have worked in highway construction, in a warehouse, and am currently helping the marketing team at Midland Metal Manufacturing. I also sell paintings on the side when I have free time. I am good with people and am a very amibtious person who loves a challenge. I am ready to work and put my skills to use.
